Admonitions

These are basically ways to highlight or callout certain bodies of text for importance.

For example:

This is the title of the note

This is the content of the note. The "meat" of what you want to amplify within the note.

This is achieved via: 

!!! note "This is the title of the note"
    This is the content of the note.
    The "meat" of what you want to amplify
    within the note.

You can also remove the title if it's not needed.

Which can be done via:

!!! note ""
    You can also remove the title if it's not needed.
